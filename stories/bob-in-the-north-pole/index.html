Böb in the South Pole

It was Saturday morning in Böb and his family's igloo.
He had just woken up after going to school on Friday.

He was very tired because he had been at school until 5 o'clock.
Böb turned 4 today and he was already 0.6 meters tall

He received a message from his friend Morgus on his mobile.
The email said that Morgus had also just woken up and wanted to hang out with Böb.

Böb sent to morgus that he would be there at 10:10. He just needed to eat breakfast and brush his teeth
but before he left his room he noticed that it was wet on the ground and unusually hot.

Böb felt a drop of water from the roof, the roof had melted because it was so hot.

Böb rushed to his parents' room to tell them but they were not home.
Böb had forgotten that they were going to the South Pole center to buy him a present.

Böb was going to call his mother Bubba, but she didn't bring her cell phone to the center.
Böb panicked and was about to run to Morgus's house but when he opened the door there was no ice but only water.

The road had melted and Bubba couldn't drive home.
Böb started swimming to Morgus' house and when he got there he knocked and told what happened.
Morgus said they have to report to the pengulice (the police).

Because the pengulice had a machine that could cool down the entire South Pole.
Böb called 221 and said what the problem was.

The pengulice said they knew about the problem but the machine had fallen into the water and
floated away to the South Pole center and there was no fast way there.

Morgus realized that he lived very close to the center and that Böb and Morgus could swim and retrieve the machine.

They started swimming to the center and finally they got there and started looking for it.
Böb found it under a block of ice that was left and they swam to the pengulice and returned it.

Then everything became normal again, Bubba and Böb's father Böebois came home.
Böb had a great birthday and he and Morgus got a medal for what they had done.
